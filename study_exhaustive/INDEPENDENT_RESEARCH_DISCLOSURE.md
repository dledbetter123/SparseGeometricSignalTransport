## Purpose

## Research Overview

On my own time and using my own resources, I have been conducting research in the field of machine learning, specifically the application of geometric methods to language modeling.

My research investigates the role of geometric structure in how language models process sequential data. The primary finding concerns a novel alternative to **rotary position embeddings (RoPE)**, which is the standard position encoding method used in most modern large language models. My method, developed through independent experimentation, has demonstrated improved performance over both RoPE and the concurrent CARoPE method across multiple model scales and evaluation settings.

I intend to publish these findings as an academic paper and would like to establish, with the support of Apple Legal, that this work is independent of and separate from my role at Apple.

## Independence of This Research

I want to clearly establish the following facts regarding the independence of this work:

- **Personal time only.** All research was conducted outside of working hours, on personal time, as a private intellectual pursuit.
- **Personal resources only.** All computation, experimentation, and development used my personal computing hardware and personal cloud accounts. No Apple hardware, servers, cloud resources, or infrastructure were used at any point.
- **No Apple proprietary information.** No Apple proprietary information, trade secrets, confidential data, internal tools, codebases, or internal research was accessed, referenced, or incorporated into this work in any way.
- **Unrelated to my role.** The subject matter of this research — geometric methods for language model position encoding — is unrelated to my current responsibilities as a Software Engineer at Apple.
- **Independent origin.** This research began as a personal research interest and was developed entirely through my own initiative, independent of any Apple project, directive, or collaboration.
- **Public literature only.** The research builds on publicly available academic literature, open-source tools, and openly published methods. All references are to public papers and standard open-source libraries.

## Request

I respectfully request that this disclosure be forwarded to Apple Legal and/or the Intellectual Property team for review. Specifically, I am seeking:

1. **Confirmation** that this independent research falls outside the scope of Apple's intellectual property assignment provisions in my employment agreement.
2. **Clearance to publish** the findings as an academic paper under my own name, with appropriate attribution to myself as the sole researcher.
3. **Written acknowledgment** (if possible) that Apple does not claim ownership of or rights to this independent work.

## Publication Intent

I am raising this disclosure now, prior to any submission, so that Apple has full visibility and the opportunity to review before any public release. I will not publish until I have received guidance from the legal team.
